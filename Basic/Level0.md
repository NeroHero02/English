## Vowels and Consonants

1. Vowels:

- A/a
- E/e
- I/i
- O/o
- U/u

- (A)ngry (E)lephant (I)n (O)range (U)nderphants ---> يمكن استعمال هذه الجملة لتذكر الأحرف

* We say the vowels with our mouths open.  (بتم قرائتها والثم مفتوح).  
2. Consonants:

- B , C , D , F , G , H , J , K , L , M , N , P , Q , R , S , T , V , W, X , Z.  

- The letter 'y' represents different sounds in different words.
- This means it is sometimes a consontants and sometimes a vowel. (يعني حرف 'y' امرات بكون حرف ساكن وامرات حرف متحرك).
- 'y' is a vowel in these words and is made with an open mouth ---> my , myth , hymn.  
- 'y' is a consonant in these words ---> beyond , yet , yippee.
